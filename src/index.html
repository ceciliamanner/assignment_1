MANDARINO DI SICILIA

  • Blu Mediterraneo Mandarino di Sicilia is a fragrance inspired by Sicily's sun-drenched landscapes, where architecture and culture merge with nature's splendor, encapsulating the Italian Arte di Vivere as a state of mind. The fragrance blends fresh, green notes of mandarin with a heart of Italian citrus, creating a lively and refreshing sensation

    • TASTING NOTES

      • Olfactive family: Aromatic Citrus
      • Top notes: Green Mandarin, Bergamot, Lemon, Blood orange
      • Heart notes: Petitgrain, Spearmint
      • Base notes: Patchouli, Cedarwood, Musk
